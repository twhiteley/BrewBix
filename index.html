Sustainable dog biscuits

Made from the by-products of brewing beer

Reduce waste by treating your dog

Breweries use tonnes of grain every week to brew beer. Once it has been used, some breweries send it to be incinerated. We are reducing waste by using this grain as the main ingredient in dog treats.
