What's in the Bix?

BrewBix are made from just 4 natural and nutritious ingredients:

Spent Grain

to help save the world

British Wholewheat flour

to give it shape and bite

Flaxseed

to help bind

Peanut butter

because dogs love peanut butter

That’s it! We've kept it as minimal as possible.

Grain from brewing you say? Isn’t that alcoholic?

The grain is only used in the mash, at the beginning of the process. This is before hops and yeast are added, so there won’t be any contact with hops or alcohol. Which is good as both are dangerous to dogs!

Learn more about the brewing process

Wait, but isn’t grain bad for dogs?

Well, no.
If your dog has a grain/gluten allergy, then yes, grain will be bad for your dog. But that’s quite uncommon. In fact, your dog is much more likely to be allergic to animal protein!

There is very little evidence to support the grain-free movement, and studies show that some grain in the diet is better than a strict high protein diet.

So is it good for my dog?

There are plenty of health benefits to the dog.

The Grain

Good for tummies and growth!
The grain is 20% plant protein which helps your dog grow and supports it’s immune system; and 70% fibre which is great for the dog’s digestion.

Wholewheat Flour

A good source of fibre for a healthy gut and a great source of energy.

Flaxseed

Contains omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids that are great for giving your dog a shiny coat!
Also high in insoluble fibre which helps keep things moving along if you know what I mean…

Peanuts

25% plant protein and a great source of energy for your dog.
Not to mention delicious. It will keep them coming back for more! #Recall

So, can these biscuits form the main part of a dog’s diet?

No. Whilst there are health benefits, these are made to be a treat. A dog needs a more varied range of nutrients than these biscuits provide for a healthy diet.

So is it Vegan?

Yes! These biscuits are totally plant-based
