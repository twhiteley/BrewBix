What beer does the grain make?

The grain we use comes from Villages, a micro-brewery in Deptford, south-east London.

Villages was started in 2016 by Archie and Louis Village. They brew a core range of sessionable ales and a lager, as well as a rotating offering of seasonal / special beers.

BrewBix are generally made from the grain used to make Whistle (a lager) or Rodeo (a hazy pale ale).
